Behind the Wheel of a Large Automobile

Once I graduated with a multi-disciplinary BFA in Electronic Imaging, I dived right into design work. It took a few years for my skills to age out. I needed to update my education, but I didn't have the money to go into school again. So I jumped bacck into the job market to rebuild my education funds and pursue some side passions in the meanwhile. That took me on a wild ride in jobs, connections and exciting travels. The last seven years of which were spent in a very unexpected role as a housing counselor. My problem solving and active listening paid off for clients. In the summer of 2017 I launched into a passion project to refit a retired transit bus into a recreational vehicle. What a journey!
